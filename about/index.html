Hi! I am Ignacio, I am from Spain and I love making games. To be precise, I love artificial intelligence in games, there is something about watching little things doing weird stuff that you told them to do…

I am passionate about game development, I can’t tell if a painting is any good, but I sure can see potential in a game. Since I was a child I have been very interested in games, but how could I imagine that does games I played were being made by people!

I remember the first time I saw a considerable amount of people playing a game I worked on, it was during the presentation of Mayhem Heroes, it was one of the best moments of my live, and it really told me that this was the way I wanted to contribute to this world.

I have vivid memories of playing Age of Empires I & II and Midtown Madness 2 on my home pc as a child. It is really interesting how back then the only thing I cared about playing a game was how to arrange my village in the coolest way or how good could I behave by following traffic on a fast paced racing game.

Now, instead of playing games like a normal person I love sitting down and watching AI agents do their thing on the games. Itry to learn the patterns and figuring out why they are executing certain behaviours.

Also, I do have a life aside from games! I am really interested in self-improvement, I do read quite a lot in the topic and I am always willing to make something to push my limits further! I have build a great discipline over the years, which I truly believe it is the main reason why I have achieved everything that I have today.

Oh, and I like cooking and cats, as separate things, that and is an important separator.
